## 🧲 Magnetic De-Tumbling (The Secret Sauce)

- Debris spinning at 50°/s? No problem.
- OVC-1 deploys a 1-Tesla magnetic field.
- Induces **eddy currents** in the debris.
- Creates drag that slows rotation in ~15 minutes.
- Then we net it. Easy.

---

## 🎬 The Capture Sequence

1. **Approach** – Match orbital velocity, close to 100m.
2. **De-Tumble** – Magnetic braking stops the spin.
3. **Net** – Launch net, capture debris.
4. **Tow** – Fire ion thrusters retrograde.
5. **Reentry** – Debris burns up. Mission complete. 🌍
