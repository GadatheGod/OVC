🧲 Magnetic De‑Tumbling: The “Stop Spinning, You Maniac” Module

How it works:

  • OVC‑1 approaches spinning debris.
  • Deploys a 1‑Tesla magnetic array.
  • Magnetic field induces eddy currents in the debris’s conductive metal.
  • These currents create a drag force (Lorentz force) that slows rotation.
